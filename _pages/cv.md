## About

I am a PhD candidate in **Accounting Information Systems at Rutgers Business School**, advised by **Professor Alexander Kogan**. My research focuses on the intersection of **auditing, artificial intelligence, and information security**, with a particular emphasis on how **large language models (LLMs)** are transforming audit processes, risk management, and enterprise governance.

My work explores topics such as:

- Cost structures and economic implications of LLM deployment in auditing
- Benchmarking and evaluation frameworks for AI-assisted auditing
- Risk management and governance for enterprise AI deployment
- Privacy-preserving technologies for secure audit data sharing
