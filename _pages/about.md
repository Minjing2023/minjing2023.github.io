This website presents the academic work and ongoing research of **Minjing**, a PhD student in **Accounting Information Systems at Rutgers Business School**. My research sits at the intersection of **auditing, artificial intelligence, and information security**, with a particular focus on how **large language models (LLMs)** and other AI technologies reshape the **economics, governance, and workflows of modern auditing**.

My current work explores questions such as:

- **How should AI systems be evaluated before they are used in regulated audit environments?**
- **What is the true economic cost of deploying intelligence in professional services?**
- **How can organizations design governance frameworks and benchmarks for trustworthy AI-assisted auditing?**

This site provides an overview of my **research papers, teaching materials, talks, and ongoing projects**, as well as resources related to **AI adoption in auditing and information systems**.
