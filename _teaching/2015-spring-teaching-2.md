I am honored to have been invited by Prof. Jun Dai to deliver a guest lecture on "Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) for LLM Deployment" at Michigan Technological University. As organizations rapidly integrate Large Language Models (LLMs) into their core operations, the traditional risk landscape has shifted. This workshop was designed to provide students with a robust framework for identifying, assessing, and mitigating the unique risks associated with generative AI, including data privacy concerns, algorithmic bias, and "hallucination" liabilities.
