I specialize in guiding aspiring accountants through their first step in the ACCA journey: Accountant in Business. My approach goes beyond rote memorization of frameworks. I focus on helping students understand why a business functions the way it does, making the transition from student to professional much smoother. By integrating current business news into my lessons, I make the theoretical frameworks of F1 come alive.
